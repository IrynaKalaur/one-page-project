Visit Madeira!

Discover the idyllic settings of this island oasis

This archipelago consists of the islands of Madeira and Porto Santo, the only inhabited islands, and also the Desertas and Selvagens islands, important nature reserves. It combines European urban sophistication with stunning natural beauty.

This archipelago is also home to the millenary Laurissilva forest, a UNESCO World Natural Heritage Site. Between the crystal-clear sea and the green mountains, there is a world of activities to enjoy in Nature, framed by magical sceneries.

The archipelago benefits from an extremely pleasant subtropical climate, recording mild temperatures all year round. In addition, the islands are bathed by crystal-clear, warm waters. Sea temperatures here vary between 18 °C and 22 °C.
